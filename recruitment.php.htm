Favourite Food Experiment

Welcome to the information and recruitment webpage for the Favourite Food Experiment.
Here You will find information regarding the study followed by a short recruitment questionnaire for You to fill in if You wish to take part.

Short Description

One of the main aims of the Favourite Food Experiment is to determine the effects of eating pleasant foods on attention and as such it does not involve any pain or discomfort. The study takes place at the Department of Experimental Psychology (near Weatherspoons), and lasts about one and a half hours. If You wish to take part, You will be provided free food and paid 7 pounds for carrying out a few tasks on a computer such as rating pictures and filling in questionnaires, as well as eating food You like.

Eligibility Criteria

The one and only real condition for Your participation is that You skip the morning or afternoon meal on the day of Your participation, so that You will have not eaten anything for at least 6 hours before the start of the experimental session i.e. so that You would be hungry.
